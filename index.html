Product Review

Amazon


Amazon is a world's largest E-commerce platform. 200 million unique monthly customers prefer making their purchases with Amazon over any other store.

What I like about Amazon
Amazon's application is simplistic and easy to use. Searching is also very efficient. There are numerous products available to choose from and not to forget that they provide international products as well. The feature that I like the most is refund on cancelled orders and the customer can choose to return a product within 7 days of delivery. There are also options for faster delivery during urgency. In COVID days AMAZON played a very important role in people's lives as people were unable to move out of their homes and home delivery of products helped them follow COVID norms.

What I feel can be improved
There are lot of times the products don't meet the quality. There are a lot of differences in the prices that are shown to different customers. People also take advantage of price drop during site maintenance. Also sometimes there is a complex data feed and it becomes difficult to choose a great product by choosing the filters given . Technical glitches are also common in the mobile application.
